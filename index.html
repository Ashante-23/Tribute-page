Dr. Rosalind Franklin

25th July 1920 - 19 April 1958

An English chemist and X-ray crystallographer whose work was central to the understanding of the molecular structures of DNA (deoxyribonucleic acid), RNA (ribonucleic acid). A role model for young and undererpresented women in STEM.
